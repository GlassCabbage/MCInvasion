Some mobs in Minecraft break into Terraria.

!!!Mod under development!!!

- Some mobs from Minecraft
- Craft a GrassBlock to meet beta mobs
- Some weapens drop from the mobs
- A pet from the underworld (Try Rainbow Dye :)
